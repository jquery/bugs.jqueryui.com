Build your Download: Add comment with what is included

Reported by: aaronbarker Owned by: paul
Priority: major Milestone: 1.7
Component: [meta] ui.downloadbuilder Version: 1.5
Keywords: Cc:
Blocked by: Blocking:
Description

It would be helpful to know what all is included in a given built bundle.

Looking at a bundle after the fact gives you no indication of what is included. When on a team, or individually trying to remember months ago when you made it, you may have no idea what all was included when you need to make a new one.

So I suggest adding a comment at the top or bottom of the field with a list of what all has been included with the name and version of each item.

Such as: 

<!--
UI Core X.X
Date Picker X.X
Tabs X.X
...
-->
