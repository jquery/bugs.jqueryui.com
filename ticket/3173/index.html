Priority: minor Milestone: 1.12.2
Component: ui.sortable Version: 1.5.2
Keywords: sortable, connected, scrolling Cc:
Blocked by: Blocking:
Description

  • two connected sortable lists
  • lists have fixed height and width and overflow auto

the bug:
